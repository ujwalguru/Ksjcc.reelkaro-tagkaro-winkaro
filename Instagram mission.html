Create Reels, Submit, & Win!

How to Register

  • Visit the official event webpage.
  • Fill out the registration form with the following details:
  • Instagram ID: Your Instagram handle.
  • Reel Link: The URL to your reel based on the Annual Day 2025-2026 celebrations.
  • Double-check the information and submit the form.
  • You’ll receive a confirmation message after successfully registering.

Terms and Conditions

  • Theme: Reels must be based on the ongoing Annual Day 2025-2026 celebrations.
  • Format: Reels must be vertical (9:16 ratio) to align with Instagram’s format.
  • Tagging: Participants must tag the college’s official Instagram account in their story.
  • Selection: Only selected reels will win prizes; not all participants will be rewarded.
  • Originality: Content must be original and adhere to Instagram’s guidelines.
  • Submission Deadline: Late entries will not be considered.

Guidelines

  • Content Focus: Highlight the excitement, performances, and key moments of the Annual Day.
  • Technical Quality: Ensure good lighting, clear audio, and proper framing.
  • Creativity: Use engaging visuals, music, or effects to stand out.
  • Length: Follow Instagram's time limit for reels.
  • Relevance: Ensure the reel aligns with the Annual Day theme.
